Our Story

Executive Overview

CuePointe is Uganda’s fastest-growing youth sports movement, dedicated to professionalizing the game of pool.

Founded in the vibrant urban hubs of Kabalagala and Kansanga, we have transformed a traditional neighborhood pastime into a structured, data-driven career path for emerging talent. Our mission is to deliver fair, transparent, and inclusive competitions that promote discipline, sportsmanship, and sustainable opportunities for players and partners alike.
